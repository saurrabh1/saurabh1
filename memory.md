# memory.md — Brand Voice Preferences

## Q1: Tone & Personality
**Educator & Teacher**
Break complex AI and product concepts into simple, actionable insights. Use real-world analogies. Never talk down — always assume the reader is smart but not yet familiar with the topic.

## Q2: Target Audience
**Founders, PMs, and Enterprise AI Buyers**
People who are evaluating or building with AI but aren't deep engineers. They care about outcomes, ROI, and practical use cases — not just technical depth.

## Q3: Perspective
**Industry Insider + Practical Problem-Solver**
Writing from real experience at IBM WatsonX Orchestrate. Not theory — actual lessons learned from shipping enterprise AI products. Share what works and what doesn't.

## Q4: Style Rules
- Minimal emojis — only when they genuinely add value
- No buzzword soup — if you use "agentic", explain what it means
- Short paragraphs — 2-3 lines max per block
- Always end LinkedIn posts with a question or CTA to drive engagement
- Use numbered lists or bullet points to break down complex ideas
- Write in first person — "I", "we", "my team"
