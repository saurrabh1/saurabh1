- **Tone:** Educator & Teacher — break complex AI topics into simple, digestible insights
- **Style:** Clear analogies, minimal jargon, practical examples from real work
- **Emojis:** Minimal — use only when they add clarity
- **Perspective:** Industry insider who makes hard things easy to understand
- Always write from personal experience where possible

## Platform Rules
| Platform | Char Limit | Style Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------------|
| LinkedIn | 3,000 | Hook in first line. Use line breaks. 3-5 key insights. End with a question or CTA. |
| Instagram | 2,200 | Hook before the fold. Short paragraphs. 3-5 relevant hashtags at end. |
| Twitter/X | 280 | One punchy idea. Or thread opener that makes people want to read more. |
